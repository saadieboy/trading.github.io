During periods of increased volatility and / or limited liquidity in the market, the value of the spread may be increased.
On the night from Wednesday to Thursday, the commission for postponing a position through the night is counted triple. For currency pairs USD / RUB, EUR / RUB and USD / CAD, the triple commission amount is taken into account when moving positions from Thursday to Friday.
For currency pairs with five decimal places, 1 point is the fourth decimal point. For currency pairs with three decimal places, 1 point is the second decimal point. The size of the commission for transferring positions overnight is indicated by the last decimal place of the quoted currency pairs.
Due to limited liquidity in the market from 23:50 to 10:00 Moscow time, the minimum spread value for USD / RUB and EUR / RUB increases to 10 kopecks.
When stop out is activated below the restrictive level, cash is compensated at the rate of closing the transaction at the margin level of 80%.
